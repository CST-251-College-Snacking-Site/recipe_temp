    Recipe Title

    Ingredients

    • Ground Turkey
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Paprika
    • Garlic Powder
    • Frozen Vegetables
    • Vegetable Oil
    • Knorr's Chicken Bouillon
    • Knorr's Liquid Seasoning

    How to cook

    1. Step 1: Place a pan on the stove and set it to a medium heat, and then add about a tablespoon of vegetable oil in the pan

    2. Step 2: Once the pan and oil are hot enough, add in your pack of ground turkey. Take a spatula and start breaking up the ground turkey

    3. Step 3: When most of the turkey is broken up, add in the salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.

    4. Step 4: Take about a small scoop of the chicken bouillon and sprinkle it all over the turkey and then add in a few squirts of the liquid seasoning

    5. Step 5: Use your spatula again to mix in everything together. Keep mixing until the turkey has a nice brown-ish color to it

    6. Step 6: Add in your mixture of frozen vegetables and mix it in with the turkey. Add a little bit of water if you want the vegetables to cook faster.

    7. Step 7: Once everything is cooked all the way through, it is ready to be served on some rice or scrambled eggs
